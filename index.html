Game Dev Week

Viborg 2020

Game Dev Week in Viborg is the leading event of Games in Denmark. Offering access to programmers, animators, artists, game designers, sound engineers, to join a vibrant community in this small town to talk about Games. Join most influenced 20+ speakers and 50+ lead companies in the market.

2020.10.04(FRI) ~ 04(SUN)

@ Plastic Art Collective, Viborg, Denmark

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to speakers from various countries about new techniques in game development

Share

Listen to indie and AAA companies talking about their production steps and learn with their mistakes.

Networking

Get opportunities to network with game developers and companies in after party.
