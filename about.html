Games Week

Viborg 2020

Game Week in Viborg 2020 is the leading event of Game Development in Denmark. Offering access to programmers, animators, artists, game designers, sound engineers, to join a vibrant community in this small town to talk about Games. Join most influenced 20+ speakers and 50+ lead companies in the market. Come together with colleagues to enjoy an environment designed to foster learning, development and great connections. During the event we will offer introductions to ensure to you key professional connections. Besides this lead meetups, networking drinks and roundtables helping to facilitate a better networking experience.

If you have any question, you can contact us using the email

contact2020@gwviborg.dk

Game Week Viborg 2020 Logo

The logo of Game Week Viborg 2020 was decided through the students at The Animation Workshop in the first edition of VAF (Viborg Animation Festival) which happens in the same time as the Game Week. So as a partner event both share the logo made by students.
